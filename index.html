About Me

I am an assistant professor at the Aoyama Gakuin University. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the University of Tsukuba under the supervision of Prof. Satoshi Iizuka and Prof. Kazuhiro Fukui. My research interests are in computer vision, graphics, image processing, and machine learning.
